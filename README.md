## What NextStepAI Is

NextStepAI provides a web interface focused on predicting student success and supporting orientation. It is aimed at university students who need early-stage guidance, particularly those beginning their first year and looking for help with academic choices.

Rather than acting as a general-purpose website, the project is organized around orientation and decision support. Its main strength is a straightforward HTML-based student experience that can be opened in a browser.
